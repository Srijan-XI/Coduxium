Polkadot

Polkadot is a multi-chain protocol focused on interoperability, shared security, and specialized parachain execution.

Overview

Blockchain Interoperability Parachains Shared Security

Polkadot enables independent chains to coordinate through a relay chain, making it useful for teams building domain-specific runtimes with cross-chain messaging needs.

Developer Foundations

  • Relay chain and parachain architecture
  • Cross-consensus messaging patterns
  • Governance and staking mechanisms
  • Substrate-based runtime development
